About Us

We are committed to reaching children and leading them to Christ by spreading the Gospel in a way they can understand and experience. Through Vacation Bible School (VBS), outreach events, and community programs, we aim to teach, encourage, and inspire children in their faith.

As we grow, we hope to expand our outreach by providing food and support to families in need, showing the love of Christ not only through words, but through action.

Our Mission

Our mission is to see souls saved and lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ. We strive to reach children everywhere, helping them grow in faith, experience God’s love, and build a strong foundation for their future.
